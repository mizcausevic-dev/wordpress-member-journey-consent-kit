# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-Back

The **WordPress Member Journey Consent Kit** shows how Kinetic Gain Embedded can turn first-party growth governance into a productized operator surface.

## Embedded relevance

- **Audience trust**: reviewed opt-in language, preference-center promises, and CRM export posture stay tied together.
- **Lifecycle safety**: onboarding, nurture, renewal, and member-digest sends can be blocked when consent evidence drifts.
- **Operator visibility**: growth, compliance, lifecycle, support, and content teams see the same member journey state.
- **Commercial path**: this can ladder into hosted preference-governance templates, member-journey audits, and embedded lifecycle consulting.

## Why it matters

Many teams treat WordPress consent and lifecycle flows as a pile of forms, plugins, and ESP settings. This repo makes the governance layer visible: which journeys are safe, where export evidence is stale, and why the next send should or should not leave the dock.
